Cloud Ascend - A Workshop by GDSC MPSTME

Cloud Ascend is a two-day workshop organized by GDSC MPSTME. The workshop aims to teach participants about Docker and DevOps with Google Cloud Console, and how to use these technologies to create and deploy scalable and secure applications.

The workshop consists of two parts: a collaborative speaker session on November 2, 2023, and a hands-on workshop on November 3, 2023. The speaker session will feature experts from the field of Google Cloud and Generative AI, who will share their insights and experiences on topics such as cloud computing, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing. The workshop will provide practical guidance and exercises on how to use Docker and DevOps tools to build, test, and deploy AI models on Google Cloud Platform.

The workshop is open to anyone who is interested in learning more about Google Cloud and Generative AI, regardless of their level of expertise or background. Participants who attend both days of the workshop will receive an exclusive certificate acknowledging their active engagement and learning in this event.

You can learn more about GDSC MPSTME and their upcoming events by visiting their website: GDSC MPSTME or their community page: Google Developer Student Clubs Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering - Mumbai .
